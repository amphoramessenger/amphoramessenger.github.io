What is this?

Amphora Messenger is an app that brings iMessage to Android, and in true Android fashion, it rocks a beautiful Material Design look. However, we did not sacrifice functionality for looks. Almost every single feature from the iPhone version of iMessage has made itself over to Android. That includes group chats, attachment support, text animations, and read indicators.
